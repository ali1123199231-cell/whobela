*A complete organic growth system for personalized romantic invitation platform*

---

## Executive Summary

Whobela solves a specific search intent gap: **"How do I ask someone out in a memorable, personalized way?"**

This strategy targets 4 distinct audience segments with unique search behaviors:
1. **Planners** - Want to create memorable experiences ("romantic date invitation")
2. **Questioners** - Unsure how to proceed ("how to ask someone out")
3. **Template-seekers** - Want inspiration and examples
4. **Occasion-focused** - Valentine's, anniversaries, proposals

**Traffic goal:** 80% organic within 12 months through keyword-targeted content + programmatic pages.
