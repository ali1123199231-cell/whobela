# Help Center

Need a hand with Whobela? Here's where to start:

* **Getting started** — Create an account, build your profile, and design your first
  invitation page from your dashboard.
* **Sharing your page** — Generate your unique link from your dashboard and share it
  directly with the person you want to invite.
* **Managing responses** — View responses, bookings, and contact details submitted through
  your page in your dashboard.
* **Billing & subscriptions** — Manage your plan, payment method, and billing history in
  account settings. See our [Subscription Terms](../09-subscription-terms.md) and [Refund
  Policy](../04-refund-policy.md).
* **Privacy & safety** — Review our [Privacy Policy](../02-privacy-policy.md), [Privacy
  Settings](privacy-settings.md), and [Safety Policy](../06-safety-policy.md).

Can't find what you need? Email support@whobela.com and we'll help directly.
