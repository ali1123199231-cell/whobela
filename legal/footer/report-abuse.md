# Report Abuse

If you've encountered a page, message, or behavior on Whobela that feels unsafe, abusive, or
violates our [Community Guidelines](../05-community-guidelines.md), please tell us.

## How to Report

* Use the in-product "Report" option where available, attaching the relevant link or
  screenshot;
* Or email support@whobela.com with a description of the issue, the link involved, and any
  supporting screenshots.

## What Happens Next

We review reports as quickly as possible and may remove content, warn a user, or suspend or
terminate an account depending on severity, in line with our [Content Removal
Policy](../07-content-removal-policy.md) and [Community Guidelines](../05-community-guidelines.md).

If you are in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services first.
