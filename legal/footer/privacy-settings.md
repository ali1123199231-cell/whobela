# Privacy Settings

Control who can see your invitation page and what information it includes.

## What You Can Control

* **Page visibility** — depending on plan, restrict your page so it's only viewable via your
  direct link, rather than being indexed or discoverable.
* **Information shown** — choose what photos, details, and contact options appear on your
  page before sharing it.
* **Booking & contact data** — review who has submitted responses or contact details through
  your page in your dashboard.
* **Marketing emails** — opt in or out of non-essential product or marketing emails; account
  and security emails cannot be disabled.
* **Account data** — request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data at any
  time — see our [Privacy Policy](../02-privacy-policy.md) for full details on exercising
  your rights.

Settings can be managed from your account dashboard. For help, contact
privacy@whobela.com.
