# Accessibility Statement

**Last updated: [Insert Date]**

Whobela is committed to making our platform usable by as many people as possible, including
people with disabilities.

## 1. Our Commitment

We aim to design Whobela to be accessible across a range of devices and abilities, and we
continually work to improve usability for everyone, including people who rely on assistive
technology.

## 2. Mobile Accessibility

Whobela is built mobile-first. We aim to ensure that core flows — creating an account,
building a page, sharing a link, and responding to an invitation — work well on small
screens, with readable text sizes, adequate touch-target spacing, and support for standard
mobile accessibility features provided by your device's operating system.

## 3. Usability Improvements

We are working toward aligning with recognized accessibility standards such as the Web
Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), including improvements such as sufficient color
contrast, keyboard navigability, descriptive alt text for images where applicable, and clear
form labeling. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we welcome feedback on areas where we
can do better.

## 4. Contact

If you experience an accessibility barrier while using Whobela, or have suggestions for
improvement, please contact support@whobela.com. We will do our best to address the issue
and respond to your feedback.
